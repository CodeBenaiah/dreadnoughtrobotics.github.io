Our Team

DREADNOUGHT ROBOTICS is a budding team of students armed with a profound interest in the field of robotics and an unfaltering willingness to learn and explore.

We, the members of Dreadnought Robotics, aim at putting to work theoretical principles and hands-on coding skills with good engineering to produce the choicest of robots. Our technical team is divided into departments of programming, electrical, and mechanical who get together to build various types of autonomous and manually controlled robots such as Robo Soccer, Robosumo, Line follower, All-Terrain Vehicle, RC Nitro Car, Drag Race, Quadcopter, Micromouse and Autonomous Underwater Vehicle from scratch to compete in competitions all over the world.

Along with the support of AUVSI Robotics Society - VITCC, the first ever Association for Unmanned Vehicle Systems International (AUVSI) Student Chapter of Asia, we are looking forward to providing knowledge to the students and contributing to the advancement of unmanned systems and robotics.

We are looking for recruits who knows their priority also understanding that the goal is more important than the role they play by finding the right place for yourself where you add the most value in this team meanwhile facing challenges which escalates your thirst for winning and finally being a catalyst who can make things happen.
Be intuitive who sense things disadvantages, advantages, opportunities, being communicative who can say they can get the work done, must be definitely passionate and capable with creativeness and willingness to grow oneself and this team and in the field of robotics and unmanned systems.
We are especially looking for recruits who brings a value to this team and ends up benefiting others and influence the course of action for this team and themselves.

Team Dreadnought Robotics has participated in many competitions, both online and offline. We took part in Waves2020 (by Anna University) where we bagged 2nd position in Robosoccer, Roboprix (by VIT University, Chennai) where we acheived runner ups in ATV, Robosoccer and Code-a-thon. We also took part in Pragyaa 2020 which is a national level competition conducted by SHRI GURU GOBIND SINGHJI INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY, Nanded. During the lockdown, TEAM CO-WARRIOR SMART SANITIZER, a group of people from our team participated in Ideonyx 1.0 by Mechonyx and acquired first position for their pitch and we had 5 teams who progressed all the way to 3rd round in Flipkart's Grid hackathon.
We do also conduct various competitions in D2C platform for our and other college students to provide help and funding for their ideas as well as the team took part in Bootcamps in various different topics, and also internships we managed to secure for few of our team members along with that we do take part in community hour conducted by Robosub teams to integrate, share and learn.
